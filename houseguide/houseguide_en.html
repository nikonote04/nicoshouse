Welcome to Nico's House in Chichibu

pets

Located in Chichibu City, about 90 minutes by car from Tokyo, "Nico's House" is an entire vacation home for rent where you can stay with your pets. No cages are required, and there are absolutely no restrictions on entering beds (low-type mattresses) or sleeping together. We warmly welcome a wide variety of pet family members, including dogs (small, medium, large), cats, rabbits, and ferrets, as natural members of the "family."

Anti-Slip, Leg-Friendly Flooring

We use flooring with a special anti-slip coating and materials with moderate texture to support paws. Please experience the layout designed to prevent injuries and reduce joint strain for senior dogs with weaker legs or energetic cats running around the room.

■ Indoor Manners & Sleeping Together

There are no restrictions on sofas or bedding. There are absolutely no restrictions on entering low-type mattresses, so you can all sleep together side-by-side just like at home. There is no obligation to use cages. If you are concerned about accidents, please wear pet diapers brought with you or use the cleaning tools provided.

warning
Never flush pet waste down the toilet
To protect pipes and septic tanks, please do not flush pet waste (feces, sheets, etc.) down the indoor flush toilet. Seal it in a bag and discard it in the dedicated trash can in the garden.
■ Heat-Shield Artificial Turf Dog Run (Garden)

A completely private dog run enclosed by a wooden fence. It is covered with special heat-shield artificial turf that prevents heat absorption even under strong summer sunlight, keeping paw pads safe. Pets can run safely off-leash. Please rinse urine with plenty of water, collect feces in a bag, and discard them in the dedicated trash can in the garden.

volume_mute
Noise Consideration During Night and Early Morning
From 20:00 to 8:00 AM, please keep your activities quiet and prevent loud barking to respect neighbors.
■ Pet Wash Station

Located in two places: right next to the entrance and in the garden (dog run). Towels are also provided for your convenience.

■ Presentation of Certificates

You do not need to show rabies or vaccine certificates at "Nico's House". However, other facilities like sightseeing spots and restaurants in the Chichibu area might request them, so we recommend bringing the originals or copies (images are fine).

🍳 Full Kitchen & Appliances

Equipped with a 3-burner gas stove, refrigerator, microwave, rice cooker, oven toaster, electric kettle, and a full set of tableware. Feel free to prepare meals for your pets or cook for your family. Please bring your own seasonings as they are not provided.

smoke_freeStrictly Non-Smoking

The entire property (all rooms, garden, wooden deck, etc.) is strictly non-smoking, including heated tobacco and e-cigarettes. Thank you for your cooperation in preventing pet health hazards and fire risks.

Famous for its Shibazakura (moss phlox). Offers a panoramic view of Mount Buko and Chichibu city. Dogs are welcome on a leash.

One of the largest dog runs in the Chichibu area. Features a spacious outdoor lawn, indoor facilities for rainy or hot days, a washing station, and a relaxing cafe for owners.

forest Chichibu Muse Park (~15 min drive) Distance: Approx. 7-8 km

A vast park with "Sky Road," a beautiful 3 km path lined with ginkgo trees. Ideal for long, relaxing walks with your dog.

water_lux Nagatoro Iwadatami & River Boating Access: Approx. 25-30 min drive (15 km)

Stroll along the magnificent natural monument rock formations on a leash. Pet-friendly river boating is available (cage required).
